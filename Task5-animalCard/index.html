TIGER

Tigers, iconic for their orange coats and dark stripes, are the largest big cats found across Asia. With diverse subspecies, known for strength and agility, they face endangerment due to habitat loss and poaching. Conservation is vital for their survival and ecological balance.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Habitat: Forests, Jungles, and Grasslands
  • Diet: Carnivora
